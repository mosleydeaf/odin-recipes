Bacon Wrapped Jalepeno Poppers

This is a quick and easy recipe. It is great for a fast snack or to serve as finger food at a party

Ingredients

  • 3 jalapenos halved and deseeded
  • 3 slices of bacon
    • Filling
  • 1/4 cup cream cheese
  • 1/4 cup shredded cheddar cheese
  • 1 clove garlic minced
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1/2 tsp pepper
  • 1/2 teaspoon paprika

Steps

  1. Preheat Air Fryer to 370to
  2. Combine filling ingredients in mixing bowl and mix thoroughly
  3. Spoon filling into jalapenos
  4. Cut slices of bacon in half
  5. Wrap filled jalapenos, try to cover starting seam
  6. Place in air fryer and cook for 8-10 minutes until bacon looks done
