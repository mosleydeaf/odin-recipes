Beef and Broccoli

Description

This is a good keto recipe for beef and broccoli. You can choose to use a honey substitue to thicken the sauce but i prefer to leave it as is and stir fry everything in the remaining marinade from the meat. It does a good job of scratching the itch for chinese food.

Ingredients

  • 1 lb top sirloin sliced
  • 1 head of broccoli chopped
  • 1 tbsp butter
  • 1 tbsp sesame seed
    • Marinade
  • 2 tbsp sesame oil
  • 2 tbsp soy suace
  • 2 cloves of garlic minced
  • 1 tsp ginger minced
  • 1/2 tsp ground black pepepr
  • 1 tsp red pepper flakes

Steps

  1. Combine marinade ingredients in a bowl and whisk thoroughly
  2. Place sliced sirloin beef in bowl, cover, and marinate overnight
  3. Place butter in skillet on medium high heat until metled
  4. Saute broccoli in skillet for 5 minutes then set aside
  5. Add sirloin to skillet, cook 2 minutes each side
  6. Add broccoli and stir
  7. Serve and garnish with sesame seed
